# Privacy Policy (隱私權政策)

**Last Updated (最後更新):** 2024-05-23

## 1. Introduction (簡介)

Mocky ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our Mocky desktop application ("App").
Mocky is designed as a **local-first** application, meaning your data stays on your device.

Mocky (以下簡稱「我們」) 非常重視您的隱私。本隱私權政策說明當您使用 Mocky 桌面應用程式 (以下簡稱「應用程式」) 時，我們如何處理您的資訊。
Mocky 是一款**本地優先 (Local-First)** 的應用程式，這意味著您的資料將完全保留在您的裝置上。

---

## 2. Data Collection and Usage (資料收集與使用)

### No Personal Data Collection (不收集個人資料)

We **do not** collect, transmit, or store any personal data, project data, or API configurations on our servers. The App operates entirely locally on your computer.

我們**不會**收集、傳輸或儲存任何個人資料、專案數據或 API 設定至我們的伺服器。本應用程式完全在您的電腦上本地運行。

### API Data & Projects (API 資料與專案)

All data you create within Mocky (including projects, routes, responses, and settings) is stored locally on your device's file system (specifically in the application's `userData` directory). We do not have access to this data.

您在 Mocky 中建立的所有資料 (包含專案、路由、回應內容與設定) 皆儲存於您裝置的檔案系統中 (具體位於應用程式的 `userData` 目錄)。我們無法存取這些資料。

---

## 3. Network Access (網路存取)

### Local Mock Server (本地模擬伺服器)

When you start a mock server in Mocky, it listens on your local network interface (typically `127.0.0.1` or `localhost`). This server is intended for your local development and testing purposes only. The App does not forward your API traffic to any external servers.

當您在 Mocky 中啟動模擬伺服器時，它會監聽您的本地網路介面 (通常是 `127.0.0.1` 或 `localhost`)。此伺服器僅供您本地開發與測試使用。本應用程式不會將您的 API 流量轉發至任何外部伺服器。

### Updates & Analytics (更新與分析)

The App currently does not include any third-party analytics or tracking tools. It acts as a standalone tool.

本應用程式目前不包含任何第三方分析或追蹤工具。它是一個獨立運作的工具。

---

## 4. Third-Party Services (第三方服務)

Mocky allows you to export documentation to HTML based on open-source libraries (e.g., Redoc). When you view these exported files or use external links, you are subject to the privacy policies of those respective services/sites. Mocky itself does not share your data with third parties.

Mocky 允許您基於開源函式庫 (如 Redoc) 將文件匯出為 HTML。當您查看這些匯出檔案或使用外部連結時，須受該服務/網站的隱私權政策規範。Mocky 本身不會將您的資料分享給第三方。

---

## 5. Security (安全性)

Since all data is stored locally, the security of your data largely depends on the security of your own device. We recommend keeping your operating system updated and using appropriate security measures.

由於所有資料皆儲存於本地，您的資料安全性主要取決於您自身裝置的安全性。我們建議您保持作業系統更新並採取適當的安全措施。

---

## 6. Changes to This Policy (政策變更)

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

我們可能會不時更新本隱私權政策。我們將透過更新本政策上方的「最後更新」日期來通知您任何變更。

---

## 7. Contact Us (聯絡我們)

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.

如果您對我們的隱私權政策有任何疑問或建議，請隨時與我們聯繫。
